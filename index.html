01
Platform Architecture · B2B SaaS

Konnect v2 Rebuild

A ground-up rebuild of Fynd's marketplace integration platform — from a legacy monolith to a modular, API-first system powering 20+ marketplace integrations and 100K+ daily transactions.

9
Production releases, zero failures
20+
Marketplace integrations
The Problem

Konnect v1 was a monolithic platform — hard to extend, difficult to debug, and slow to onboard new marketplaces. Each new integration required significant engineering effort with no shared primitives.

The Approach

Defined a modular, extension-based architecture using FDK (Fynd Developer Kit) for integration lifecycle management, Temporal for workflow orchestration on long-running tasks, and a Kafka-based message bus for event-driven sync. Consolidated all Konnect services into a single monorepo — konnect-admin, konnect-console, konnect-gateway, konnect-message-bus, KLAS, and Nightwing.

The Outcome

Platform now supports 20+ live marketplace integrations, Lazada across 5 APAC countries, and handles 100K+ transactions daily. Delivered 9 production releases with structured DRI-based deployment coordination across multiple engineering teams.

02
AI Product · Seller Tooling

Konnect Copilot

A GPT-powered assistant embedded directly inside Konnect — guiding sellers through channel setup, product mappings, and analytics queries in real time, without needing to raise a support ticket.

18%
Reduction in support tickets
50%
Faster prototyping
The Problem

Sellers and internal admins were raising high volumes of support tickets for tasks that were documented — but the documentation was scattered and hard to navigate during active workflows.

The Approach

Wrote the one-pager proposal and drove the product from concept to ship. Managed documentation indexing strategy — robots.txt, sitemap configuration — to ensure the model was trained exclusively on Konnect documentation, improving response accuracy. Designed the assistant's UX to surface contextual guidance without breaking the seller's primary workflow.

The Outcome

18% reduction in seller and admin support tickets. 50% improvement in prototyping speed for new feature validation. Became the foundation for further AI-native features within the platform.

METRO Wholesale Platform

India's first cash-and-carry wholesale brand digitization — delivered on Jio Commerce Platform as one of 3 PMs. From zero to a fully operational B2B e-commerce platform serving Kirana merchants across 31 stores.

The Problem

METRO Cash & Carry India had no digital commerce presence. Kirana merchants were managing wholesale ordering manually — no platform, no promotions engine, no merchant data.

The Approach

Defined and delivered a full B2B platform feature suite: user hierarchy, dynamic and ladder pricing, contract pricing per merchant, multi-MOP payment integration (JioOnePay, RONE Wallet), and a behavioral cohort engine for personalized delivery charges, payment restrictions, and recommendations. Built a Kafka pipeline for 3.6M+ records/day with alerting that reduced downtime by 94%.

The Outcome

Launched with 45K merchants, 7,634 brands, 70K SKUs, and 500+ orders. Promotions engine drove 11% cart size increase and 14% reduction in low-value orders. Kafka alerting reduced platform downtime by 94%.

Beyond the Work

Range

I believe people should have range. Not because it looks good on paper — but because moving across unfamiliar things teaches you how to learn, how to fail without stopping, and how to carry instincts from one domain into another. That's also what makes a better PM.

🇯🇵

Japanese

Studying at JLPT N5 level. Not because I needed to — because the structure of a completely different language forces your brain to work differently. And I'm open to relocating to Japan.

🏊

Swimming & Surfing

I was scared of water. I learned to swim anyway. Then I learned to surf. Fear of something is rarely a good reason not to try it — that's a rule I apply at work too.

✒️

Ink Drawing

Black pen art — fineliner work, detailed and slow. It's the opposite of how I work in product: no undo, no iteration, just commitment to each line. I find that balance useful.

🎹

Piano

Currently learning. I'm a complete beginner — which is a humbling and necessary experience for anyone who spends most of their day being the person with the answers.

🚗

Driving

Learned to drive in my late twenties. Another thing I kept deferring until I didn't. Sometimes the most useful skill is just deciding to finally do the thing.

🎨

Watercolour (Failed)

I tried watercolour painting and genuinely couldn't get it. I'm leaving this in because I think being honest about where you failed is more interesting than pretending everything worked.

"I failed at watercolour and kept going. That's the same instinct I bring to hard product problems."
