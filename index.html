Konnect v2 Rebuild

The Approach

Defined a modular, extension-based architecture: FDK for integration lifecycle, Temporal for long-running workflow orchestration, Kafka for event-driven sync. The Outcome

20+ live integrations. Lazada across 5 APAC countries. 100K+ transactions/day. Full platform overhaul shipped without major failures.

CheckApp IPD Workflow

Hospital IPD operations were manual and fragmented, with no system tying together admission, nursing, EMR, procedures, and discharge.

35%
Increase in customer satisfaction

METRO Wholesale Platform
